Privacy Policy

Learn how we protect your personal information.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information while using the Lost & Found platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our platform, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal details such as your name and email address.
  • Details of the items you report as lost or found, including images and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate the matching of lost and found items.
  • To improve the user experience and provide better service.
  • To contact you regarding items you have reported, if necessary.

3. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data. We use secure servers and encryption to ensure your information remains private.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or when you explicitly consent.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data. For any such requests, please contact us using the information provided below.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please review this page periodically for changes.
