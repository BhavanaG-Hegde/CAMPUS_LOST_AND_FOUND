About Lost & Found

Helping you reunite with your lost belongings.

Who We Are

Lost & Found is a platform built to connect people with their lost and found items. Whether you've lost something valuable or found an item on campus, we are here to simplify the process and make the experience stress-free. Our mission is to create a trustworthy and accessible platform for everyone.

What We Do

With our easy-to-use interface, you can report lost items, post found items, and browse through reports to locate your belongings. We also provide weekly insights to show how effective the system is at helping users.

Simple Reporting

Effortlessly report lost or found items with detailed descriptions and images.

Powerful Search

Quickly find items using keywords, categories, and filters.

Lost and Found Items

Browse reported lost and found items, track their status, and view location details.
